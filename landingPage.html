    Who We Are

    About Our Organization

    Founded in 2010, Helping Paws is a volunteer-driven non-profit dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming stray and abandoned animals.

    Our team of caring volunteers works around the clock — providing food, shelter, and medical attention — until every animal finds a warm, loving forever home.
